Abidjan, My Favorite City

About Abidjan

Abidjan is the economic capital of Ivory Coast. It is a modern and dynamic city known for its skyscrapers, lagoon, and vibrant lifestyle.

Why I Love Abidjan

I love Abidjan because it is full of energy, opportunities, and cultural diversity. The city offers a unique mix of tradition and modern life.
